AGRICARE: PULSES PRICE AND PRODUCTIVITY INSIGHTS

This webpage provides insightful analytics on the prices and productivity of key pulses such as Gram, Tur, Urad, and Moong dal across different Indian states. The interactive graphs below are designed to help government officials monitor trends, evaluate regional variations, and make data-driven decisions regarding agricultural policies and resource allocation.

The analysis will assist in addressing challenges such as price volatility, productivity management, and regional differences, which can inform future policy-making, subsidy adjustments, and planning for sustainable agricultural development.
