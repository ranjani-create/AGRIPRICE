About Us

Welcome to Commodity Price Prediction, an initiative by the Government of Consumer Affairs to provide real-time insights on vegetable and commodity prices. Our platform offers tools to analyze price trends, market news, and visualize regional price variations with heat maps, enabling better decision-making for consumers and government bodies.

Our mission is to ensure transparency and provide a user-friendly interface that helps both consumers and policymakers stay informed about market fluctuations and the factors that affect them.
