Demand and Supply curve for Essential Commodities

The Government of India actively monitors the demand and supply of essential commodities to ensure equitable access and price stability for all citizens. The Department of Consumer Affairs regularly assesses the market trends for critical items such as food grains, pulses, and edible oils.

Through efficient management of supply chains and maintenance of adequate buffer stocks, the government prevents market shortages and excessive price volatility. This strategy plays a key role in maintaining food security, ensuring public welfare, and supporting India's commitment to a resilient and sustainable market environment.
